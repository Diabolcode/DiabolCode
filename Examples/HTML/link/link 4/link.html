October war results 1) The restoration of full sovereignty over the Suez Canal, and the recovery of all lands in the Sinai Peninsula.

2) Reclaiming part of the Syrian Golan Heights, including the city of Quneitra, and returning it to Syrian sovereignty.

3) Breaking the myth that the Israeli army is invincible, which the military leaders in Israel used to say.

4) The war also led to the return of navigation in the Suez Canal in June 1975 AD.
Military results of the war 1) Israel failed in its assessment of the strength of the neighboring Arab countries and their military capabilities, as well as in its analysis of the information obtained.

2) It has also been proven that the fortified Bar-Lev line is nothing but a fortified defensive line that can be breached with good planning, determination and high-level training.

3) The destruction of a large number of the Israeli forces and the infliction of high casualties on the Israeli forces.

4) The integrated system of the Egyptian air defense, despite being established under the pressure of Israeli air attacks, during the war of attrition, was able to neutralize the Israeli air force.

5) The Egyptian offensive plan achieved a strategic surprise, and thus succeeded in paralyzing and confusing the Israeli leadership, at various levels.
